Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

SubTrack helps users track recurring subscriptions, renewal dates, and estimated monthly or yearly spending.

Data we process

  • Subscription names entered by the user
  • Subscription prices entered by the user
  • Billing cycle and renewal dates entered by the user
  • Local technical data required to enable reminders and app functionality

How data is used

  • To display subscriptions and calculate monthly and yearly spending summaries
  • To show local reminders before upcoming renewals
  • To enable premium entitlements purchased through Google Play

Where data is stored

  • Subscription data is stored locally on the user's device using Room database storage
  • The app does not upload subscription records to an external server

Notifications

  • The app may send local notifications when an upcoming renewal is near
  • Notification permission is used only for subscription reminder functionality

Payments and Premium

  • Premium purchases are processed through Google Play Billing
  • Payment card or billing details are not collected or stored by SubTrack

Data sharing

  • SubTrack does not sell personal data
  • SubTrack does not share subscription records with third parties, except as required to complete purchases through Google Play

Data deletion

  • Users can edit or delete subscriptions inside the app
  • Removing the app may also remove locally stored app data, depending on Android system behavior

Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the developer contact information shown on the SubTrack Google Play listing.
